Intermediate Fitness Plan

Workout Plan

Day 1: Strength Training

Deadlifts, Bench Press, Pull-Ups, Leg Press (4 sets of 8-10 reps each)

Day 2: Cardio and Core

30 minutes of HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training)
Core exercises: Planks, Russian Twists, Bicycle Crunches (3 sets of 15 reps each)

Day 3: Rest

Rest and recovery day. Focus on stretching and relaxation techniques.

Day 4: Cardio and Flexibility

45 minutes of running or cycling
Yoga or Pilates for flexibility and balance (30 minutes)

Day 5: Endurance and Cardio

Long-distance run or bike ride (60 minutes)
HIIT workout (20 minutes)

Diet Plan

Day 1

Breakfast: Protein smoothie with spinach, banana, and protein powder
Lunch: Grilled chicken breast with quinoa and roasted vegetables
Dinner: Baked salmon with brown rice and steamed broccoli

Day 2

Breakfast: Greek yogurt with granola and mixed berries
Lunch: Turkey and avocado wrap with whole grain tortilla
Dinner: Grilled shrimp with sweet potato and asparagus

Day 3

Breakfast: Oatmeal with sliced almonds, honey, and strawberries
Lunch: Tuna salad with mixed greens and olive oil dressing
Dinner: Grilled steak with quinoa and roasted vegetables
