Advanced Fitness Plan

Workout Plan

Day 1: Strength and Power

Power cleans, Squats, Bench Press, Pull-Ups (5 sets of 5 reps each)

Day 2: High-Intensity Cardio and Core

Sprints, Box Jumps, Kettlebell Swings, Ab Rollouts (4 sets of 15 reps each)

Day 3: Rest

Rest and recovery day. Focus on stretching, yoga, or meditation.

Day 4: Hypertrophy and Endurance

Dumbbell Lunges, Dumbbell Rows, Push Press, Planks (4 sets of 8-12 reps each)

Day 5: Cardio and Agility

Interval Running, Jump Rope, Agility Ladder Drills, Bodyweight Exercises (5 sets of 20 seconds on, 40 seconds off)

Diet Plan

Day 1

Breakfast: Protein smoothie with kale, banana, protein powder, and almond milk
Lunch: Grilled chicken breast with quinoa, mixed greens, and avocado
Dinner: Baked salmon with sweet potato, steamed broccoli, and asparagus

Day 2

Breakfast: Greek yogurt with chia seeds, granola, and mixed berries
Lunch: Turkey and cheese sandwich with whole grain bread, spinach, and tomatoes
Dinner: Grilled shrimp with brown rice, roasted vegetables, and a side salad

Day 3

Breakfast: Oatmeal with sliced almonds, honey, and mixed berries
Lunch: Tuna salad with mixed greens, olives, feta cheese, and olive oil dressing
Dinner: Grilled steak with quinoa, roasted vegetables, and a side of guacamole
