Beginner Fitness Plan

Workout Plan


Day 1: Cardio

30 minutes of brisk walking or jogging

Day 2: Strength Training

Bodyweight squats, push-ups, and planks (3 sets of 10 reps each)

Day 3: Rest

Relax and allow your body to recover.

Day 4: Cardio

30 minutes of cycling or swimming

Day 5: Flexibility

Yoga or stretching exercises for 20 minutes

Diet Plan


Day 1

Breakfast: Whole grain toast with avocado
Lunch: Grilled chicken with mixed vegetables
Dinner: Baked salmon with quinoa

Day 2

Breakfast: Greek yogurt with berries
Lunch: Turkey sandwich with whole grain bread
Dinner: Stir-fried tofu with brown rice

Day 3

Breakfast: Oatmeal with banana and nuts
Lunch: Spinach salad with grilled shrimp
Dinner: Grilled chicken breast with sweet potato
